Help

  • You get to meet someone new. You've got skills–and it's awesome to be able to share those with people. Doing what you love is a great activity when meeting someone new because you're at your best.
  • You get to practice. Sometimes you need experience to prove your talents to employers or customers. There's no better way to get experience than by offering it freely to your community.
  • Can I charge? No! Money's not allowed. However, while it's not encouraged, trading favors is allowed–just keep the mindset that you're giving and getting freely rather than exchanging.
  • Trust your instincts If a person, situation or profile seems unsafe for any reason, move on. Don’t worry about seeming rude. Communicate clearly with others and take care of yourself.
  • Communicate through Favor.trade Don’t give out your phone number and email address until you meet and feel comfortable with a new person. Use the messaging system within the website to communicate.
  • Review profiles carefully Take the time to carefully review member profiles. Read what members say about themselves and what other members have said about them.
  • Leave Feedback Let other Favor.traders know about your experiences with the people you meet. Be honest and clear. You can do this by leaving reviews for other members to see.
Need something else? Shoot us an email at info@favor.trade.
